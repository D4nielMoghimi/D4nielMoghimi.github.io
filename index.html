About Me

I am a computer scientist working as a research assistant toward a doctorate degree in the electrical & computer engineering (ECE) department at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI). Before that, I received a master of science degree from computer science (CS) department at WPI. My research interests include computer security, side channels and applied cryptography.

I work with my advisors Prof. Berk Sunar and Prof. Thomas Eisenbarth as a member of the Vernam Group. You can find a list of my academic publications here or at my google scholar profile.
